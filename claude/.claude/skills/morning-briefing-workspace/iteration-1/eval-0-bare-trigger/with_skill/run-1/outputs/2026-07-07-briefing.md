## Tech & AI

- **DeepSeek is building its own AI chip** — Reuters reports the Chinese startup is developing in-house silicon, and the news is dragging AI/chip stocks lower worldwide. If a top Chinese lab can wean off Nvidia, the "compute moat" assumption behind a lot of AI valuations gets shakier — worth watching for client conversations about vendor lock-in and training-cost trajectories. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/chinas-deepseek-developing-its-own-ai-chip-sources-say-2026-07-07/)
- **Microsoft puts $2.5B into an AI consulting business** — Microsoft is investing directly in AI consulting, continuing the trend of vendors moving into services. A hyperscaler competing (and partnering) in the delivery layer changes the competitive map for independent data/AI consultancies. [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/technology/ai/articles/microsoft-furthers-trend-invests-2-152708926.html)
- **Samsung flags a 19x jump in Q2 operating profit (~$58.4B), shares fall anyway** — Record HBM-driven results beat estimates, but capex concerns and the DeepSeek chip story sent the stock and the wider chip sector down. The market is now punishing even great AI-infrastructure earnings, a sentiment shift from the past two years. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/samsung-estimates-19-fold-rise-q2-operating-profit-beating-expectations-2026-07-06/), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/07/samsung-electronics-preliminary-second-quarter-profit-hits-fresh-high.html)

Recent context still landing with customers: the Databricks storage ecosystem push (governing data wherever it lives, Qumulo private preview slated for this month) and HITRUST compliance coverage across all three clouds from Data + AI Summit. July platform release notes are rolling out in staged fashion. [Databricks blog](https://www.databricks.com/blog/announcing-databricks-storage-ecosystem-governing-enterprise-data-estate-wherever-it-lives), [release notes](https://docs.databricks.com/aws/en/release-notes/product/2026/july)
- **Consulting-adjacent signal: SI ecosystem consolidating around Databricks** — EY took Databricks' FS Partner of the Year for a third cycle, and Databricks Ventures took a stake in boutique partner v4c.ai's Series A. Databricks is increasingly funding its own delivery ecosystem — both an opportunity and a competitive squeeze for unaffiliated consultancies.
